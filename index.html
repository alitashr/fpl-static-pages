Our Mission

To take our future in our hands by improving education.

We aim to inspire youths (FunMakers) to go out into the world and do something constructive. We focus them on an important issue: Education. We show you can do something useful with just good ideas and a good heart. We train them to play games that give young children joy and inspire curiousity. Our aim is NOT to teach children. Instead it is to inspire them to want to learn.
