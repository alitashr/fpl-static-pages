Funmaker of the Week

Anjila Karki

(2022-11-11)

Working in a Fun Play Learn lead me to personal growth building new skills improve my communication and teamwork abilities. Its a great way to meet new people and have fun while playing games.The ballon cart game was my absolute favourite throughout the session because it is fun to play and help children to strengthen their motor skills and pin wheel was the most interesting game among children. The kids were so curious and asked if they could make it bigger one? Their question, curiosity always brought happiness and was incredibly motivating. I loved taking on leadership roles and had the chance to enhance my leadership skills there.
